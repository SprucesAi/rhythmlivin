Rhythm

Cleaning Checklist

Basic Cleaning

  • Wipe and dust all surfaces to remove dust and dirt.

  • Clean all bathroom surfaces, including toilets, sinks, and mirrors.

  • Ensure all bathrooms are stocked with toilet paper and hand soap.

  • Clean all kitchen surfaces, including countertops and sinks.

  • Empty all waste bins and replace liners.

Full Cleaning List

  • Wipe and dust all surfaces to remove dust and dirt.

  • Clean all bathroom surfaces, including toilets, sinks, and mirrors.

  • Ensure all bathrooms are stocked with toilet paper and hand soap.

  • Clean all kitchen surfaces, including countertops and sinks.

  • Return all glassware and plates to the kitchen for cleaning.

  • Sweep or vacuum all floor surfaces to remove dirt and debris.

  • Mop all floor surfaces to ensure they are clean.

  • Empty all waste bins and replace liners.

  • Clean the glass on all exterior doors to remove smudges and fingerprints.

Scope for the Big Monthly Clean

  • Dust all high surfaces including tops of shelves, cabinets, and light fixtures.

  • Clean all windows both inside and outside to remove all smudges and dirt.

  • Use a carpet cleaner to deep clean all carpeted areas.

  • Thoroughly sanitize all surfaces, including desks, countertops, and touchpoints.

  • Remove dust and debris from all air vents to ensure clean air flow.

  • Polish all wooden furniture to maintain its appearance and longevity.

  • Thoroughly clean all kitchen appliances including the fridge, microwave, and oven.

  • Organize all storage areas to ensure everything is in its place and easily accessible.

  • Remove dust and dirt from all light fixtures to ensure maximum brightness.

  • Ensure all fire extinguishers are in good working condition and easily accessible.
